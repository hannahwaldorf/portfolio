A little about me

Balancing data with curiosity

I'm an advertising professional with a passion for bringing ideas to life through thoughtful execution, creative problem-solving, and a keen eye for detail.

I'm currently a Digital Account Specialist at Paramount, where I manage the end-to-end execution of multi-platform advertising campaigns for national brands — from launch and optimization to reporting and cross-functional collaboration. I enjoy bringing structure to complex projects and making sure every detail comes together seamlessly.

While I love the analytical side of advertising, I'm equally drawn to creativity. I believe the best solutions come from balancing data with curiosity, and I'm always exploring new trends in marketing, technology, and design to keep learning and growing.

Outside of work, you'll usually find me behind a camera, working on a crochet project, painting, experimenting with new recipes, tending to my ever-growing collection of houseplants, or spending time with my bunny and dogs.
